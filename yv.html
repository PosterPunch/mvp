Jake Paul, known as "The Problem Child," is an American professional boxer, social media personality, and entrepreneur. Born on January 17, 1997, in Cleveland, Ohio, Paul first gained fame on the now-defunct video-sharing platform Vine before transitioning to YouTube where he amassed millions of followers.

Paul made his professional boxing debut in 2020 and quickly established himself as a serious contender in the cruiserweight division. With an impressive record of 11 wins (7 by knockout) and only 1 loss, Paul has become one of the most talked-about figures in combat sports.

Known for his explosive power, athleticism, and relentless work ethic, Paul has dedicated himself to the sport of boxing, training with some of the best coaches in the business. His fights consistently draw massive pay-per-view audiences, making him one of the most valuable athletes in combat sports today.
