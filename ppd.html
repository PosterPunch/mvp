FEAR NO MAN

Jake Paul vs. Mike Perry - Event Recap

July 20, 2024 | Amalie Arena, Tampa, Florida

In a spectacular night of boxing presented by MVP, Jake Paul faced off against the dangerous Mike Perry in what promised to be an explosive encounter. The event delivered on all fronts, with thrilling matchups from top to bottom.

Main Event: Paul vs. Perry

Jake "El Gallo" Paul vs. "Platinum" Mike Perry

Jake Paul (27 years)
TKO (Round 6, 1:12)
Mike Perry (32 years)

In front of a sold-out crowd of 17,500, Jake "El Gallo" Paul showcased his boxing prowess against "Platinum" Mike Perry. From the opening bell, Paul dominated, delivering knockdowns in the first and second rounds. Despite Perry's legendary toughness and resilience, the bout concluded in the sixth round when Paul unleashed a decisive combination, prompting the referee to halt the contest at 1:12.

Post-fight, Paul expressed respect for Perry's tenacity: "Mike's as tough as they come. I hit him with everything and he kept coming. This was the perfect test before Tyson." Paul then set his sights on his upcoming bout with Mike Tyson, scheduled for November 15.

Co-Main Event: Serrano vs. Morgan

Amanda Serrano vs. Stevie Morgan

Amanda Serrano
TKO (Round 2, 0:38)
Stevie Morgan

Amanda Serrano celebrated her 50th professional fight with a commanding performance against Stevie Morgan. Demonstrating her renowned power, Serrano secured a TKO victory just 38 seconds into the second round, reinforcing her position as a dominant force in women's boxing.

"Fifty fights and I'm still hungry," Serrano stated in her post-fight interview. "I want all the champions at 126 and 130 pounds. Let's make history."

Main Card Results

Featured Bouts

Lucas Bahdi vs. Ashton Sylve
KO (Round 6)

In a dramatic turnaround, Lucas Bahdi delivered a stunning knockout against previously undefeated Ashton "H2O" Sylve in the sixth round, marking a contender for 'Knockout of the Year'.

Corey Marksman vs. Tony Aguilar
MD (76-76, 77-75, 78-74)

In their lightweight rematch, Corey Marksman emerged victorious via majority decision in a closely contested battle.

Julio Cesar Chavez Jr. vs. Uriah Hall
UD (59-55, 59-55, 58-56)

Chavez Jr. used his superior boxing skills to outpoint UFC veteran Uriah Hall over six rounds.

Ariel Perez vs. Dane Guerrero

Kicking off the night, Ariel "Golden Hulk" Perez thrilled the hometown crowd with a third-round knockout over Dane Guerrero.

"MVP continues to deliver the most exciting events in combat sports. Fear No Man was another historic night for our organization, showcasing both established stars and the next generation of talent."

Post-Event Analysis

The event proved to be another successful venture for MVP, with the Paul-Perry fight exceeding expectations in terms of action and drama. Perry's toughness won him new fans despite the loss, while Paul demonstrated improved technique and patience in breaking down a dangerous opponent.

Serrano's dominant performance set up potential super fights in the women's featherweight division, while Bahdi's knockout of Sylve was immediately declared one of the best of 2024.

Looking Ahead

With the success of "Fear No Man," MVP looks forward to its November mega-event featuring Jake Paul versus Mike Tyson. The organization also announced plans for Amanda Serrano to challenge for another world title later this year.
