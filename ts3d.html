Katie Taylor and Amanda Serrano Set for Historic Trilogy Fight

Streaming Live on Netflix from Madison Square Garden

July 11, 2025

One of boxing's most iconic rivalries returns to center stage as Katie Taylor and Amanda Serrano prepare for their highly anticipated trilogy bout. Announced by Most Valuable Promotions and Netflix, Taylor vs. Serrano III will stream live globally on Friday, July 11, from the legendary Madison Square Garden — and will be available at no extra cost to all Netflix subscribers.

This showdown marks a major milestone: the first-ever all-women's professional boxing card at Madison Square Garden, reinforcing MVP's commitment to elevating women's boxing on the world stage.

A Rivalry That Changed the Game

This will be the third chapter in a fierce and historic rivalry. The first fight in April 2022 broke barriers as the first women's boxing match to headline Madison Square Garden, earning recognition as Fight of the Year by Sports Illustrated and Event of the Year by The Ring.

The rematch took place during Paul vs. Tyson last November and was watched by over 108 million viewers. Taylor narrowly retained her titles with a controversial majority decision win (95–94 across all three judges' scorecards), sparking intense debate among fans and experts alike.

Now, the stage is set for the ultimate decider — back where it all began, with Taylor's undisputed super lightweight titles once again on the line.

"This is more than just a fight — it's a celebration of women's boxing," said MVP co-founders Jake Paul and Nakisa Bidarian. "This all-women's card marks a major step forward and the beginning of even bigger things for female fighters."

Fighter Statements

Katie Taylor

"I'm 2-0 against Amanda, but this is a very special rivalry that delivers every time we step into the ring. The atmosphere at MSG for our first fight was unforgettable, and I expect nothing less this time. After the massive audience from the last event, we're ready to give fans another incredible night of boxing."

Amanda Serrano

"I promised my fans we'd complete the trilogy, and now we're back at MSG, live on Netflix, for the world to see. I'm disappointed Katie didn't agree to make it 12 three-minute rounds as we had discussed — our fans deserve that. But make no mistake, every time we fight, it's war. And this time, I'm walking out with the win I deserve."

Breaking New Ground

Taylor and Serrano will receive record-breaking purses, setting a new financial benchmark for women in the sport. MVP continues to lead the charge for equity in boxing, using this event to spotlight female athletes and inspire the next generation of champions.

The event will feature an all-women's undercard, showcasing rising stars in women's boxing and providing unprecedented exposure for female fighters on one of combat sports' biggest stages.

Don't miss one of the most anticipated fights in modern boxing history — streaming live on Netflix from Madison Square Garden on Friday, July 11.
