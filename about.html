About Us

Most Valuable Promotions (MVP) is on a mission to build the premier global boxing platform by captivating the next generation through dynamic digital storytelling and world-class live events — all powered by a fighter-first mindset.

Co-founded in 2021 by global influencer Jake Paul and sports executive Nakisa Bidarian, MVP has already redefined the sport by delivering some of the most iconic fights in recent history. From the historic Jake Paul vs. Mike Tyson bout to the epic Katie Taylor vs. Amanda Serrano rematch, MVP made history by hosting the first-ever live professional sporting event on Netflix, reaching a staggering 108 million live viewers.

With a bold vision and unmatched reach, MVP has ignited a new era of boxing fandom — bringing millions of new fans into the sport and spotlighting the next generation of boxing superstars.
