About The Fighter

Nicknamed "The Problem Child," Jake Paul made a sensational professional debut in January 2020, securing a first-round TKO against AnEsonGib. Since then, he has achieved an impressive 11-1 record, with notable victories over former MMA stars such as Ben Askren, Tyron Woodley (twice), Anderson Silva, and Nate Diaz.

In November 2024, Paul secured a unanimous decision win over boxing legend Mike Tyson, further solidifying his status in the boxing world. Known for his dynamic presence and commitment to the sport, Paul continues to be a compelling and influential figure in modern boxing.

Born in Cleveland, Ohio, Paul now resides and trains in Dorado, Puerto Rico. Standing 6'1" with a 76" reach, the orthodox fighter has shown continuous improvement since transitioning from social media fame to professional boxing.
