Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2023

Most Valuable Promotions Ltd is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. We handle all personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 1998 (DPA).

Your Rights

At any time, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct or update your information
  • Request the deletion of your data

Fight Pass Members can log into their accounts to view and update their personal information.

Email subscribers can manage preferences and access personal data via the links found at the bottom of our emails.

Information We Collect

We collect personal data directly from you, which may include:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email address
  • Date of birth

For any purchases or transactions, payment data is securely processed and stored by trusted third-party providers using encrypted SSL technology. We do not store your payment details directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Personalize our website and communications
  • Improve and protect our services and content
  • Send you relevant marketing emails (only where permitted)

Data Storage

All personal data is stored on secure servers located in the United Kingdom.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and help us understand how visitors interact with our site. Cookies are small files stored on your device that track browsing behavior.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at:

Email: info@mostvaluablepromotions.com

We may ask for identification to verify your request.

Most Valuable Promotions Ltd reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Please check our website periodically for the latest version.
