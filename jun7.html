Jake Paul vs. Mike Tyson & Katie Taylor vs. Amanda Serrano Rescheduled

Mega-Event Now Set for Friday, November 15 at AT&T Stadium

June 07, 2024

The highly anticipated heavyweight fight between Jake Paul and Mike Tyson and the most anticipated rematch in women's boxing history, Katie Taylor vs. Amanda Serrano, is rescheduled for Friday, November 15, 2024 at AT&T Stadium in Arlington, Texas.

Rescheduled Event Details:

Main Event: Jake Paul vs. Mike Tyson

Co-Main: Katie Taylor vs. Amanda Serrano II

New Date: Friday, November 15, 2024

Location: AT&T Stadium, Arlington, TX

Broadcast: Live globally on Netflix

GET TICKETS

"Our team has worked diligently with all parties involved to reschedule this monumental fight to a date that ensures both Jake Paul and Mike Tyson are fully prepared, with equal training time, as well as allowing us to keep the event at AT&T stadium in the midst of the Cowboys season," said Nakisa Bidarian, Co-Founder of Most Valuable Promotions. "Paul vs. Tyson is the most cross generational sporting event in history, and we are committed to providing fans worldwide with this unforgettable night of boxing, live globally on Netflix."

"Although we had to postpone the fight, I will resume training shortly. I am thankful to the medical staff that treated me and to MVP, Netflix, and AT&T Stadium for working diligently to find the best reschedule date for all parties," said Mike Tyson. "While we have a new date, the result will be the same no matter when we fight. Jake Paul is getting knocked out."

Ticket Information:

Existing Tickets: All previously purchased tickets will be honored for the new date (November 15). No action is required.

Refunds: Those unable to attend the rescheduled date are eligible for refund at their original point of purchase until July 8, 2024.

How to Request Refund: Email dallascowboys@seatgeek.com with subject line "Paul vs. Tyson"
