MVP: Most Valuable Prospects 9

October 11, 2024 • Coliseo Roger Mendoza • Caguas, Puerto Rico

Event Details

On Friday, October 11, Most Valuable Promotions delivered a thrilling night of boxing at Coliseo Roger Mendoza in Caguas, Puerto Rico. The action-packed card was streamed globally on DAZN, with a host of exciting matchups showcasing up-and-coming talent.

In the main event, Jan Paul Rivera secured an impressive victory over Andy Beltran in a scheduled 8-round super featherweight bout. Rivera, the local favorite, dominated the fight, earning a unanimous decision victory with the judges scoring it 79-73, 80-72, and 80-72. With this win, Rivera remained unbeaten, improving his record to 11-0 and claimed the WBO "Youth" Featherweight Title.

The co-main event featured a thrilling bantamweight clash, where Krystal Rosado outpointed Perla Lomeli in a 6-round bout. Rosado improved to 5-0, with the judges scoring it 59-55, 60-54, and 60-54 in her favor.

Another standout performance came from Elijah Flores, who earned a unanimous decision over Omar Rosario in a hard-fought 8-round welterweight battle. Flores maintained his undefeated record, moving to 9-0 with the judges scoring it 79-73, 77-75, and 77-75.

Kicking off the main card, Alexis Chaparro stopped Steven Kirkwood in the first round of their middleweight bout, improving to 3-0, 3 KOs. The official stoppage came at 2:49 of Round 1.
